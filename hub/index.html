Utilities

SFX Tools

Small browser-based tools for document workflows. Files are processed locally in your browser where possible.

JSX JSX Renderer Render React JSX files in the browser and export an interactive HTML file. Open MD Markdown to PDF Convert Markdown documents into PDFs with live preview, page size, font size, and margin controls. Open PDF PDF Watermark Add repeated or centered text watermarks to PDF files entirely in the browser. Open SIGN PDF Fill & Sign Open a PDF or DOCX, place dates and titles, draw your signature, and export a signed PDF. Open
